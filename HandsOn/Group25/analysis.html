Resource Naming Strategy

Domain: http://www.madridDogs.es/

Path: http://www.madridDogs.es/info/

Class: http://www.madridDogs.es/info/ontology#[className]

Property: http://www.madridDogs.es/info/ontology#[propertyName]

Data: http://www.madridDogs.es/info/resource/

Individual: http://www.madridDogs.es/info/resource/[className]#[ID]
