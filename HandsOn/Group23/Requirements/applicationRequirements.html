Application Requirements

Bike Locator(Name in Progress)

Datasets used: APARCABICIS_2021.csv | bases_bicimad.csv

Use of data: Display the location of electrical bike stations and outdoor parking zones

Description: Electric Bike rental locations of BiciMad, and outdoors bicycle racks
(It doesn't include: indoor areas, historical and forest parks)

Interface: It would show a map of Madrid (city) with a search bar for addresses and two toggle buttons
which would show either electric stations or public racks for bikes (even both at the same time)
